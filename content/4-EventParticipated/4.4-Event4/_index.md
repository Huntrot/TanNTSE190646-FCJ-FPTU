## Report on AWS Cloud Mastery Series #1 – Generative AI with Amazon Bedrock

### Purpose of the Event
- Update knowledge on **Generative AI on AWS** and learn how to deploy AI solutions with **Amazon Bedrock**.  
- Practice various **prompting techniques**: zero-shot, few-shot, and chain-of-thought (CoT).  
- Improve ability to apply GenAI to personal projects and professional workloads.  
- Network with the AWS community and professionals in Vietnam.  

---

### Speakers
- **Lam Tuan Kiet** – Sr DevOps Engineer, FPT Software  
- **Danh Hoang Hieu Nghi** – AI Engineer, Renova Cloud  
- **Dinh Le Hoang Anh** – Cloud Engineer Trainee, First Cloud AI Journey  

---

### Key Highlights

#### Generative AI with Amazon Bedrock
- Introduction to **Foundation Models** and how to choose the right model for specific use cases.  
- Hands-on demonstration of **Prompt Engineering Techniques**:  
  - **Zero-shot** – model produces answers without examples.  
  - **Few-shot** – guidance through sample examples.  
  - **Chain-of-Thought (CoT)** – step-by-step reasoning for better accuracy.  
- **Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG):** enhance answers using external data sources.  
- **Bedrock Agents:** automate multi-step AI tasks using models and tools.  
- **Guardrails:** enforce content safety and compliance.  

#### Mini Kahoot Review
- An interactive quiz to reinforce key knowledge on prompting and GenAI workflows.  

---

### Personal Experience & Lessons Learned
- Impressed by the practical application of **zero-shot, few-shot, and CoT** techniques.  
- Hands-on Bedrock demonstration provided a clear view of **end-to-end AI workflow development**.  
- Kahoot review improved knowledge retention during the workshop.  
- Networking sessions offered valuable opportunities to connect with AWS experts and practitioners.  

---

### Outcomes Achieved
- Mastered practical prompting techniques for building chatbots and AI-driven applications.  
- Understood how to integrate **Foundation Models** and **RAG** into real-world solutions.  
- Improved proficiency in quickly deploying Generative AI on AWS.  
- Gained ideas for using quizzes to reinforce knowledge in team training or workshops.
